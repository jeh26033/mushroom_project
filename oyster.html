A mushroom is classified as the fleshy, spore making section of a fungus, usually created above the surface of the earth. They appear in many shapes, sizes, flavors, and colors. Some are delicious, and can create wonderful food, either on their own or as accompaniments to other foods. There are mushrooms that defy our expectations for what a mushroom should look like, and each has it's own unique flavor profile.

Others have medicinal benefits, and others can out-right kill you or poison you. To be safe, it's best to purchase your Fungi from a good source, like a farmers market or a supermarket, and probably not out in the forest, unless you really trust your fungal knowledge. This site covers many common mushrooms you'll find in such places,and serves as an introduction to the Wonder World of Mushrooms!
